PRIVACY POLICY
Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 8 December 2024

1 Introduction
Welcome to Manchester City Club. We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, attend matches, or interact with our services. By using our website, services, or attending our events, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2 Information We Collect We may collect the following types of personal information: Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, date of birth, and other information necessary for registration and participation in our services. Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and payment method information when purchasing tickets, merchandise, or other products. Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, services, or social media platforms, including browsing activity, device information, IP address, and location data. Event Attendance Data: Information about your attendance at our matches, events, and other activities, such as ticket purchases and fan engagement.

3 How We Use Your Information We use your personal information for the following purposes: To process ticket and merchandise purchases and deliver products or services. To provide you with updates about club activities, events, news, and promotions. To communicate with you regarding your membership, subscription, or account. To analyze website traffic and usage patterns to improve our website and services. To ensure the security of our website, services, and events. To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

4. How We Share Your Information We may share your personal information with: Third-Party Service Providers: We may share information with vendors or service providers who assist in managing our website, processing payments, delivering products, or handling marketing campaigns. Business Partners: We may collaborate with partners for joint marketing initiatives or events, and we may share certain information with them for those purposes. Legal Authorities: We may disclose information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes, or in response to lawful requests by public authorities. In the Event of a Merger or Acquisition: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

5 Data Security
We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you send to us.

6. Your Rights and Choices You have the following rights regarding your personal information:
Access: You can request to view the personal information we hold about you. Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information. Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions. Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website.
These technologies collect information such as IP addresses, browser types, and browsing behavior. You can control cookie settings through your browser, but disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features on our website.

8. Children’s Privacy
Our website and services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

ABOUT CLUB

Manchester City Football Club is a professional football club based in Manchester, England, that competes in the Premier League, the top flight of English football. Founded in 1880 as St. Mark's (West Gorton), they became Ardwick Association Football Club in 1887 and Manchester City in 1894.
